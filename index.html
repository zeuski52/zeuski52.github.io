Biography

Charles Darwin was born on Fegruary 12th, 1809 in Shrewsbury, England.[1] He is most famously known for his theories and contributions to evolutionary biology.[2] His biggest claim is that all species on earth evolved from a common ancestor.[3] One of Charles Darwins most famous works was his book "theory of evolution" which provided evidence to support his theory on the origins of the human species.

Famous Quote

Although much remains obscure, and will long remain obscure, ... I am convinced that Natural Selection has been the main but not exclusive means of modification.

- Charles Darwin
